Big Data With Databricks

By utilizing Apache Spark's parallel processing capabilities, we can perform complex transformations on massive datasets in real-time, integrating streaming analytics, MLflow for model tracking, and advanced hyperparameter tuning.
Our focus is on optimizing data pipelines, ensuring data quality, and implementing robust ETL processes to drive business intelligence and predictive analytics.

Player Behaviour Analysis & Game Recommendations

What makes a game truly addictive?
To find out, I built a recommendation system that suggests games to users based on their play patterns and purchase history. Using SQL for EDA and Python’s ALS algorithm, I engineered advanced behavioural features to personalise recommendations. The entire workflow ran on Databricks, making it scalable and lightning-fast. The result? A smarter way to boost player engagement in a crowded market.

Healthcare Trials Data Analysis

What is shaping the future of clinical research?
To find out, I analysed a vast dataset of US clinical trials using PySpark SQL on Databricks, uncovering patterns in trial types, conditions, durations, and their evolution over three decades. Through advanced data wrangling and exploratory analysis, I mapped out trends in diseases like diabetes, cancer, and obesity — revealing how research responds to public health crises, medical breakthroughs, and global events. The insights offer a data-driven lens into the dynamic world of clinical research strategy.
